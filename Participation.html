Participation Policy

We would like to express our gratitude to you personally and to your organization for its significant contributions to the development of the UAE real estate sector and for enhancing its global competitiveness. Dubai is currently experiencing rapid growth in the real estate industry, with global megatrends driving substantial changes in the real estate landscape.

In this context, and with the support of the property industry and the regional and global investment organizations, and the investors and the media, the Dubai land Department is pleased to present the "Women-Empowerment in Property and Real Estate Event" at 9am-1pm on November 11, 2024, at the Palazzo Versace Dubai, Managed by Datamatix.

The event will bring together women investors and leaders, decision makers and industry experts, to discuss opportunities and strategies for modernizing, strengthening and increasing women role in investment in the real estate sector. The events provide a platform for strategy development, comprehensive knowledge sharing, and forecasting the future of property development and the women leader’s empowerment and involvement in this strong industry.

Therefore, the events attendance is by invitation and you may get your seat booked by the event sponsors do not hesitate to contact us in case if you have any question in this regard via the official contact email:info@womenindubairealestate.com

Thank you for your continuous efforts and support.

Best Regards

The Attendees Are from the Bellow Industries

  • Businesswomen and Investors
  • Business Leaders who are looking to invest in real estate industry.
  • Women Leaders and Decision makers from Government involved with the real estate sector.
  • Women Leaders and Executives from Investment Companies, Fund Management Companies
  • Women Leaders from Consultants, Developers, Contractors, Architects and Consultants
  • Women Leaders from Insurance Companies, Asset Management Companies
  • Women Leaders Economic Development Authorities, City Promotion Agencies & Authorities
